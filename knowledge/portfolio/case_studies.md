# Portfolio & Case Studies - ZenCro Digital

Here is a selection of projects delivered by ZenCro Digital, showing real outcomes and technologies used.

---

## Case Study 1: FitLife Gym (Web & WhatsApp Automation)
- **Client**: FitLife Gym (Pune, India)
- **Challenge**: The gym was losing members due to slow reply times for schedule inquiries and membership packages.
- **Solution**: ZenCro Digital built a sleek website showcasing facilities, trainers, and dynamic schedule tables. Additionally, we integrated a WhatsApp bot for automatic membership package sharing and booking gym visits.
- **Results**: +40% increase in monthly membership queries and 20+ hours saved per week for the gym receptionist.
- **Stack**: React, CSS Modules, Supabase, WhatsApp Business API.

---

## Case Study 2: Bright Minds Academy (School Web Portal)
- **Client**: Bright Minds School (Mumbai, India)
- **Challenge**: Parents and prospective students struggled to find school notices, academic files, and admission inquiries.
- **Solution**: We created a modern Next.js educational portal featuring parent notice boards, downloadable resources, virtual photo galleries, and an online admission request module.
- **Results**: 120+ admission inquiries captured online in the first month; streamlined parent communications.
- **Stack**: Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, Node.js, Vercel.

---

## Case Study 3: Spicy Delight Restaurant (Digital Dining Platform)
- **Client**: Spicy Delight Bistro
- **Challenge**: Relying on high-commission aggregators like Swiggy/Zomato was hurting margins, and phone call table bookings were error-prone.
- **Solution**: We built a high-converting, mobile-responsive restaurant website featuring an interactive visual menu, table booking form, and a WhatsApp-based order-ahead dashboard.
- **Results**: 30% increase in direct-to-restaurant table bookings; reduced order errors to nearly zero.
- **Stack**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Supabase.

---

## Case Study 4: Vogue Wear (D2C E-Commerce Brand)
- **Client**: Vogue Wear Apparels
- **Challenge**: Sluggish loading times on Shopify and lack of customization options.
- **Solution**: Built a headless Next.js e-commerce storefront with customized product filters, instant checkout buttons, and integrated Razorpay & Stripe payment gateways.
- **Results**: Reduced page load times by 65%, causing a 18% lift in checkout conversion rate.
- **Stack**: Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Supabase, Razorpay, Stripe.

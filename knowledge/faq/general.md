# General FAQs - ZenCro Digital

### Q1: How long does it take to build a website?
- **Answer**: 
  - A single landing page takes **3-5 days**.
  - A standard corporate business website takes **10-15 days**.
  - An e-commerce store or custom web application takes **3-5 weeks** depending on the complexity of features and integrations.

### Q2: What is your payment structure?
- **Answer**: We usually operate on a **50% advance deposit** and **50% upon project completion** before deployment. For large custom software, we break it into milestones (e.g., 30% advance, 40% demo milestone, 30% final signoff).

### Q3: Who owns the domain, hosting, and code?
- **Answer**: The client owns all assets. Once the final payment is cleared, we transfer the domain, hosting access, and full code repository ownership to the client.

### Q4: Do you offer post-launch support?
- **Answer**: Yes, every project includes **30 days of free maintenance** (covers bug fixes, server monitoring, and simple text edits). After that, we offer monthly retainer packages starting at ₹2,500 ($35) / month.

### Q5: Can you help integrate social media and WhatsApp?
- **Answer**: Yes, we specialize in full digital automation. We can link Instagram profiles, set up automatic comment-to-DM flows, connect official WhatsApp APIs, and map all inquiries into a CRM spreadsheet or database.

### Q6: Where is ZenCro Digital located?
- **Answer**: We are based in **Maharashtra, India**. However, we operate remotely and serve clients globally.

# Services: AI & Process Automation

ZenCro Digital helps businesses eliminate repetitive tasks, engage customers 24/7, and close more leads through intelligent automated workflows, voice agents, WhatsApp APIs, and CRM integrations.

## Automation Services

### 1. AI Chatbots
- **What it is**: Intelligent, context-aware conversational bots integrated into websites, apps, or social channels.
- **Capabilities**: Knows company profile, answers FAQs, collects lead information, and qualifies users before routing to a human.
- **Benefits**: 24/7 support, instant reply times, and zero missed customer inquiries.

### 2. AI Receptionist / Voice Agents
- **What it is**: AI voice bots capable of calling customers or taking inbound calls with natural language processing.
- **Capabilities**: Book appointments, qualify inbound support leads, answer primary business queries, and route complex cases to live representatives.
- **Benefits**: Cuts call center costs and guarantees that no customer call goes unanswered.

### 3. WhatsApp Automation
- **What it is**: Custom automation using the official WhatsApp Business API.
- **Capabilities**: Automated welcome messages, interactive quick-reply buttons, order/shipping updates, abandoned cart reminders, and chatbot integrations.
- **Benefits**: Achieves 98%+ open rates and connects directly with customers on their preferred platform.

### 4. CRM & Database Automation
- **What it is**: Integrations between your website, ads, or chatbot and tools like HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, or Supabase.
- **Capabilities**: Auto-create leads, assign tags, trigger automated notification alerts to salespeople, and update pipeline stages.
- **Benefits**: Eliminates manual copy-pasting and ensures instant follow-up on leads.

### 5. Email & Lead Automation
- **What it is**: Automated email nurturing campaigns and lead validation chains.
- **Capabilities**: Auto-send email brochures upon signup, drip-feed product information, and automatically follow up with cold leads.
- **Benefits**: Keeps your brand top-of-mind and moves leads down the sales funnel automatically.

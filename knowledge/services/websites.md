# Services: Web Design & Development

ZenCro Digital builds premium, high-performance, responsive websites tailored to specific industry verticals. All our sites feature state-of-the-art designs, optimized SEO, blazing-fast speed, and modular structures.

## Website Types & Categories

### 1. Business & Corporate Websites
- **Target**: B2B companies, consulting agencies, local businesses, service providers.
- **Features**: Service listings, client testimonials, team members, contact/inquiry forms, interactive FAQs, and blog integration.
- **Value**: Establishes digital authority and converts traffic into qualified business leads.

### 2. School & Educational Institution Websites
- **Target**: Schools, colleges, daycare centers, coaching centers.
- **Features**: Notice boards, online admission inquiry forms, event calendars, galleries, curriculum details, teacher directories, and student/parent login portals.
- **Value**: Simplifies administration and communication between staff, parents, and prospective students.

### 3. Gym & Fitness Center Websites
- **Target**: Fitness centers, crossfit boxes, yoga studios, personal trainers.
- **Features**: Membership tier display, trainer profiles, class scheduling guides, BMI calculators, client success transformation sliders, and membership signup call-to-actions.
- **Value**: Simplifies membership acquisition and displays class schedules transparently.

### 4. Restaurant & Cafe Websites
- **Target**: Fine dining restaurants, cafes, food chains, bistros.
- **Features**: Visual digital menus, online table reservation forms, integration with delivery platforms (or direct order systems), location/contact details with embedded maps, and client reviews.
- **Value**: Boosts seat reservations and allows customers to explore menus before visiting.

### 5. Hotel & Hospitality Websites
- **Target**: Hotels, motels, luxury resorts, homestays/Airbnbs.
- **Features**: Room galleries, amenity descriptions, seasonal pricing tables, booking request forms, local attraction maps, and virtual tours.
- **Value**: Drives direct bookings, bypassing high OTA commission fees.

### 6. Professional Portfolio Websites
- **Target**: Designers, photographers, writers, consultants, freelancers.
- **Features**: Project grids, client list showcase, case studies, resume download, and booking links (Calendly, etc.).
- **Value**: Displays work beautifully to secure jobs and projects.

### 7. Landing Pages
- **Target**: Ad campaigns, product launches, lead magnets.
- **Features**: Single-page high-converting design, clear hero section with value proposition, benefits checklist, social proof, and a single, focused lead-capture form.
- **Value**: Highly optimized to maximize return on ad spend (ROAS) from Google/Meta ads.

### 8. E-Commerce Stores
- **Target**: Retail brands, direct-to-consumer (D2C) businesses, digital product sellers.
- **Features**: Product catalogs, search & filter, cart & checkout workflows, payment gateway integrations (Razorpay, Stripe, UPI), order management dashboard, discount coupon code configurations, and inventory status tracking.
- **Value**: A complete online shop ready to process payments and handle deliveries.

## Tech Stack & Standards
We build using **Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and Supabase** for custom enterprise applications. For simple content sites, we can also deploy optimized CMS solutions. All sites include:
- **Mobile Responsive Design**: Fits perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.
- **SEO Ready**: Proper schema markup, meta tags, heading hierarchies, and fast loading.
- **Domain & Hosting Setup**: Configuration of DNS, hosting on Vercel/AWS, and free SSL certificates.
- **Website Maintenance**: Ongoing monitoring, backups, and content updates.
